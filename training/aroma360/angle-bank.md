# 😤 Pain Point


## The Refill Roulette

Hook: "Measuring oil into a diffuser shouldn't feel like a chemistry experiment. (It doesn't, with Pro-Pods.)"

Supporting Evidence: Traditional refillable diffusers require pouring, measuring, and cleanup. Pro-Pod snap-in design eliminates all of it.

Target Segment: Tech-Forward, Self-Care/Wellness

Format Suggestions: Side-by-side comparison reel (messy pour vs. clean snap), UGC frustration/resolution

Status: Untested
