# GOV+ — Angle Bank

> A living library of creative angles to test in performance ads. Each card contains a unique strategic angle derived from customer reviews, Reddit community research, deep market analysis, and the GOV+ messaging blueprint. Organized by motivator segment.
>
> Last updated: March 2026 | Angles: 18 | Status: Initial build

---

## How to Use This Bank

1. **Pick a motivator segment** relevant to your campaign goal and season
2. **Choose an angle card** within that segment
3. **Stack it** with a characteristic segment from the Audience Segment Plan for targeting
4. **Test 3–5 hooks** from each angle before declaring a winner or loser
5. **Rotate angles** every 2–4 weeks to prevent creative fatigue
6. **Tag results** back to this bank — mark angles as Tested / Winner / Fatigued

### Awareness Stage Key
Each angle targets a specific awareness stage (from Box Scaling framework):
- **Symptom** — Largest audience, lowest conversion. Surface-level frustration.
- **Problem** — Audience knows the pain. Agitate and show why it matters.
- **Solution** — Audience is looking for a fix. Position the solution category.
- **Product** — High intent. Features, benefits, proof points.
- **Offer** — Smallest audience, highest conversion. Urgency and risk reversal.
