# Gov+ Compliance Guide

## CRITICAL: Check ALL briefs against this list before finalizing

## Forbidden Words

- **Government** — Never use as noun describing GOV+
- **Official** — Never use
- **Hack** — Never use
- **Always** — No absolutes
- **Never** — No absolutes
- **100%** — No absolutes (except "100% Accuracy Guarantee" with disclaimer)

## Forbidden Claims

- ❌ "Get your documents instantly"
- ❌ "Get your documents faster with GOV+"
- ❌ "All online" (unless the ENTIRE service is fully online)
- ❌ "Apply online" (for services requiring appointments)
- ❌ Government logos on ads

## Allowed Alternatives

- ✅ "Apply instantly"
- ✅ "up to 4 weeks"
- ✅ "as little as 2 weeks"
- ✅ "Apply faster"
- ✅ "Online application"
- ✅ "Start with a simple online application"
- ✅ "Follow simple step by step instructions"

## Competitor References

- ✅ **Allowed:** "traditional filing methods"
- ❌ **Not Allowed:** Naming specific competitors (H&R Block, TurboTax, etc.)

## Storytelling

- ✅ Hypothetical prospect stories are allowed
- ✅ "Imagine if..." scenarios are allowed
- ✅ First-person testimonial style (with actual testimonials) is allowed

## Product-Specific Compliance Notes

### PAS (Passport)
- **Renewal:** Fully online — can say "apply online"
- **New/Lost/Damaged:** Requires appointment — use "simple online application + follow instructions" or "start online, then visit acceptance facility"

### TSA (PreCheck)
- **Renewal:** Fully online — can say "apply online"
- **New Applications:** Requires appointment — use "online application + appointment booking" or "we book your appointment for you"

### TAX (Taxes)
- **Three Tiers:** DIY, Pro Review, Pro — match messaging to tier
- **Guarantees:**
  - "Maximum Refund Guarantee" — requires disclaimer
  - "100% Accuracy Guarantee" — requires disclaimer ($10,000 max reimbursement)
- **Expert Claims:** Can say "human tax expert support" for Pro Review and Pro tiers only

## Required Disclaimers

### Visual Disclaimer (for all video/ad creative)
Must appear on screen or on landing page:
> "GovPlus is a private online software technology company not affiliated nor endorsed by any Government or State agency"

### Tax Guarantee Disclaimers
- Max Refund: Include legal disclaimer about conditions
- Accuracy: $10,000 max reimbursement for math errors

## Quick Compliance Checklist

Before finalizing any brief:
- [ ] No forbidden words used
- [ ] No forbidden claims made
- [ ] Appointment-required services don't say "all online"
- [ ] Competitors are not named
- [ ] Required disclaimers noted for production
- [ ] Tax tier messaging matches actual tier features
