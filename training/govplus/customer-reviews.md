## Objections to Address (From Negative Reviews)

### Price Concerns
- "I just paid $222 for a TSA pre check renewal when the real government charges $59"
- "They charge a very high fee for forms that are available online for free"
- "I was not prepared to pay out so much but in the long run well worth it"

**Counter Messaging:** Focus on value of time saved, convenience, expert review, avoiding mistakes

### Government Affiliation Confusion
- "GOV+ is NOT part of the US government"
- "The branding and layout resemble official government portals"

**Counter Messaging:** Never imply government affiliation (compliance rule)

### Processing Time
- "I just pray that the standard shipping process arrives within 91 government days"
- "I have not been able to get what I wanted"

**Counter Messaging:** Use ranges like "up to 4 weeks" / "as little as 2 weeks"

### Photo Upload Issues
- "Tried to upload photo but can't get it"
- "Initial issue with taking the photo with my phone"

**Counter Messaging:** Highlight support availability

### Upsells
- "I did not like the extra things offered"
- "Everytime you enter information they want you to agree to up charges"

**Counter Messaging:** Focus on core value, transparent pricing

## Demographic Insights

### Seniors (55+)
- Highly value convenience
- Appreciate "from home" messaging
- Time-saving resonates strongly
- "Stressless" is a key word

### First-Time Users
- Need reassurance it's legitimate
- Value step-by-step guidance
- Social proof important

### Busy Professionals
- Time is primary concern
- "No trips to the office" messaging works
- Speed matters

### Travelers
- TSA PreCheck for airport convenience
- Passport renewal before trips
- Urgency around deadlines
