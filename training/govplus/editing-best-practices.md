# Editing Best Practices

## Native Editing

### Tools
- Use **CapCut** (integrates native tools/effects)

### Platform-Native Fonts
- **SF Pro** — Instagram native font
- **TikTok Sans** — TikTok native font
- **Apple emoji pack** — Feels organic

### Goal
Make ads look like **organic content**, not polished ads. Native = higher trust, better performance.

---

## Contrast Clip to Clip

### Core Principle
Always have **something happen** in each clip. Never let two consecutive clips feel the same.

### Ways to Create Contrast

| Dimension | Variation |
|-----------|-----------|
| Content type | Entertaining vs educational |
| Subject | Product vs people vs environment |
| Shot size | Closeups vs wide shots |
| Camera movement | Panning vs fixed |
| Location | Inside vs outside |
| Stability | Handheld vs tripod |
| Color | B&W vs color, saturated vs muted |
| Audio | Music changes, sound effects (use sparingly) |

---

## Fast Pacing

### Clip Frequency
- Target **~25 clips per minute** (~2-3 seconds per clip)
- Keeps attention, matches platform behavior

### Voiceover Speed
- Speed up voiceover **1.1x-1.2x** (imperceptible to viewer)
- Tightens pacing without sounding unnatural

### Cut Ruthlessly
- Remove dead air
- Remove pauses
- Remove filler words ("um", "like", "so")

### Text Pacing
- Fast text reveals (single word or few words at a time)
- Don't leave text on screen too long

---

## Focus on the Beginning

### Why It Matters
- More people see the **start** than the end
- Thumb-stop happens in first 1-3 seconds
- Most drop-off happens early

### Prioritization Rule
**Incredible first 7 seconds + weak last 20 seconds > "meh" throughout**

### Application
- Put best editing in first 7 seconds
- Put most engaging visuals in first 7 seconds
- Hook must land immediately
- Save "good enough" footage for later in the ad

## Fixing Common Errors

### Audio Issues
- **Adobe Podcast Enhance Speech** — Free AI tool for cleaning up audio
- Removes background noise, improves clarity

### Flipped Clips
- Watch for **backwards text and logos** when flipping clips
- Check brand elements, signs, shirts with text

### Eyeline Matching
- Keep eyes in **consistent position** across cuts
- Jarring when eyeline jumps around frame

### Exposure & Color
- **Bump saturation** — iPhone footage tends to be muted
- Adjust exposure for consistency across clips
- Match color temperature between clips

---

## Safe Zone Guidelines

### Why It Matters
Platform UI covers parts of the frame. Text, faces, and products must stay visible.

### Recommendations
- Keep captions, text, faces, products **in the middle** of frame
- Account for UI overlays (buttons, share icons, usernames)
- **9:16 vertical** → auto-crops to 4:5 or 1:1 for feed placements
- Have creators leave space above their head for text overlays

### Platform-Specific Notes
- Instagram: Bottom ~15% often covered by CTA buttons
- TikTok: Bottom ~20% and right side icons
- Facebook: Varies by placement

## Audio Best Practices

### Music Selection
- Use **Meta Sound Collection** (royalty-free, platform-approved)
- Match mood to content (uplifting for solutions, tense for problems)
- Drop music at hook moment for emphasis

### Voiceover
- Clear, conversational tone
- Speed up 1.1x-1.2x
- Remove filler words and pauses

### Sound Effects
- Use sparingly for emphasis
- Whoosh for transitions
- Pop for text reveals
- Don't overdo it

---

## Captions

### Always Include Captions
- 85%+ of social video is watched without sound
- Captions increase watch time and comprehension
- Exception: Handwriting/b-roll mashup frameworks where script is covered by handwriting

### Caption Style
- Large, readable font
- High contrast (white text with black outline or background)
- Centered or bottom-third positioning
- Keep in safe zones

---

## Quick Reference Checklist

Before exporting:
- [ ] Best footage in first 7 seconds
- [ ] ~25 clips per minute pacing
- [ ] Voiceover sped up 1.1x-1.2x
- [ ] Dead air and filler words cut
- [ ] Clip-to-clip contrast throughout
- [ ] Text/faces in safe zones
- [ ] Captions included (unless handwriting framework)
- [ ] Exposure and color matched across clips
- [ ] Audio cleaned and balanced
- [ ] No backwards text from flipped clips
