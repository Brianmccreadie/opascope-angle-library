## PAS (Passport)

### Product URLs
- https://govplus.com/start/passport

### Unique Value Props
- Get your passport all from home
- Expedited processing and shipping available
- Online application (no paper forms)
- Online passport photo (vs pharmacy)
- Electronic signature
- Customized mail-in materials
- No printer needed
- No checks needed
- No envelope needed
- Real-time tracking
- Expert review
- Instant friendly support

### Process: Passport Renewal
1. Fill out a simple online form
2. Receive your customized mail-in materials
3. Send your documents
4. Wait 8-12 weeks average (expedited 6-8 weeks)
5. Get your passport in the mail

### Process: New Passport / Lost / Damaged
1. Fill out a simple online form
2. Receive your customized mail-in materials
3. Go to the designated passport office
4. Drop your documents
5. Wait ~6 weeks average
6. Get your passport in the mail

### Compliance Note
- **Renewal:** Fully online — can say "apply online"
- **New/Lost/Damaged:** Requires appointment — cannot say "apply online"

## TSA (PreCheck)

### Product URLs
- https://govplus.com/start/precheck

### Unique Value Props
- Online application
- Avoid application delays and mistakes
- Real-time tracking
- Immediate support (phone, chat, email)
- Expert review
- GOV+ books your appointment for you (new applications)

### Process: TSA PreCheck New
1. Fill out a simple online form
2. GOV+ books your appointment for you
3. Go to your appointment
4. Pay government fees on premises
5. If approved, get your KTN number by email

### Process: TSA PreCheck Renewal
1. Fill out a simple online form
2. Pay government fees online with GOV+
3. Get your TSA PreCheck® renewed

### Compliance Note
- **Renewal:** Fully online — can say "apply online"
- **New:** Requires appointment — use "online application + appointment"

## TAX (Taxes)

### Product URLs
- https://govplus.com/start/taxes

### Three Tiers

#### DIY Filer
- **Best for:** Simple tax situations
- **Key benefit:** File yourself in under an hour, max refund
- **Value props:**
  - Max refund guaranteed*
  - As little as 40 minutes
  - Low/no cost, no hidden fees
  - 100% accuracy guarantee**
  - Unlimited live support

#### Pro Review (DIWM - Do It With Me)
- **Best for:** Simple to slightly complex situations
- **Key benefit:** File yourself with expert guidance
- **Value props:**
  - Support from credentialed Tax Pro before you file
  - Keep control with professional guidance
  - Transparent pricing
  - 100% accuracy guarantee**

#### Pro (DIFM - Do It For Me)
- **Best for:** Complex tax situations
- **Key benefit:** Credentialed Tax Pros handle everything
- **Value props:**
  - End-to-end filing by credentialed Tax Pro
  - Upload docs at once
  - Live/chat access to your Tax Pro
  - 100% accuracy guarantee**

### Required Disclaimers
- *Max refund: Must include legal disclaimer about conditions
- **Accuracy: $10,000 max reimbursement for math errors

### Tax Season Messaging Themes
- **Pre-season:** Awareness, trust building
- **Early (Jan):** "File first, refund faster"
- **Mid (Feb-Mar):** "Your forms are ready"
- **Late (Apr):** Urgency, "last chance", deadline
