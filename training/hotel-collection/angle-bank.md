# Hotel Collection — Angle Bank

# 😤 Pain Point


## The Candle Graveyard

Hook: "You have 4 half-burned candles collecting dust. There's a better way."

Supporting Evidence: Avg US household has 5+ unused candles. Candles burn unevenly, produce soot, require attention.

Target Segment: Self-Care/Wellness, Tech-Forward

Format Suggestions: UGC-style video, static with product swap visual

Status: Untested
