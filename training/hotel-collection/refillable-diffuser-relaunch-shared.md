# Refillable Diffuser Relaunch 2026 | Hotel Collection & Aroma360

## Executive Summary (Internal Use)

We are reintroducing our refillable diffuser experience across both brands, beginning with a hard onsite switch on February 23.

- **Hotel Collection** → Studio Scent Diffuser
- **Aroma360** → Mini 360 SL

This initiative is driven by customer feedback and demand for refillable flexibility.

> **This is not a recall, not a safety issue, and not a pre-order.**

All external messaging should center around:
- "Back by Popular Demand"
- "We Heard You"
- "Refillable Oils"
- "Refillable Diffuser"

A separate **Customer Upgrade Program** will be managed exclusively by Customer Service via 1:1 outreach alongside the CRM team.
