## Likely Winning Patterns (Hypothesis Based on Category Research)

Based on competitor analysis and category trends, the following are likely high-performing creative patterns for Hotel Collection:

### Hook Patterns That Likely Work:
1. **"What does [Hotel Name] smell like?"** — Curiosity + hotel nostalgia
2. **"Everyone asks what my home smells like"** — Social proof + compliments angle
3. **UGC unboxing / first reaction** — Authentic discovery moment
4. **Room transformation** — Before (plain room) → After (styled + diffuser running)
5. **"This is my $50 luxury secret"** — Affordable luxury framing

### Visual Patterns That Likely Work:
1. Diffuser in a beautiful, styled room setting
2. Close-up of mist dispersing from diffuser
3. Guest reaction / compliment moment
4. Hotel lobby → home comparison
5. Scent oil pour / product detail shots

### Format Patterns:
1. Short-form UGC (15-30s) for cold traffic acquisition
2. Lifestyle B-roll mashups for broader awareness
3. "Day in my life" with diffuser as a featured moment
4. Product demo with scent description overlay
