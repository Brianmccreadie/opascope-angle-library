# Hotel Collection — Audience Segment Plan
## Studio Pro & Entry-Level Diffuser Acquisition Focus

## How This Plan Works

This segment plan breaks the Hotel Collection audience into two building blocks for performance creative:

- **Motivator Segments** — The pain points and desires that drive purchase. These set the overall messaging theme for a brief.
- **Characteristic Segments** — Who the person is (demographics, behaviors, situations). These add specificity and targeting precision.

**Segment Stacking:** The most effective creative stacks one Motivator + one Characteristic for high-resonance targeting.
Example: Social Validation (motivator) × The Entertainer (characteristic) = "Your guests will notice the moment they walk in."

All size estimates are directional, based on the U.S. home fragrance market (~$7.2B in 2025, ~130M U.S. households). Hotel Collection's addressable market is estimated at ~40-60M U.S. households that purchase home fragrance products annually. Segments overlap — a single customer may fit multiple segments.

## Segment Stacking Strategy

The most effective creative stacks one Motivator (the messaging theme) with one Characteristic (the person).

### Highest-Priority Stacks for Acquisition

|-----------|---------------|----------------|----------------|
| Social Validation | The Entertainer | "Your guests will notice the moment they walk in." | Primary acquisition — strongest review-validated angle |
| Social Validation | The Home Curator | "The finishing touch your curated space is missing." | Primary acquisition — largest addressable audience |

| Hotel Nostalgia | The Hotel Traveler | "The Aria lobby. The 1 Hotel suite. Your living room." | Highest intent — lowest education needed |

### Acquisition Priority Order (75% of spend):
1. **Social Validation × The Home Curator / The Entertainer** (broadest reach, review-validated)
2. **Failed Alternative Frustration × The Candle Quitter** (high volume, active pain point)
3. **Affordable Luxury × Young Nesters / The Home Curator** (entry-level price alignment)
4. **Pet Odor Management × Pet Parents** (underserved niche, strong differentiator)
5. **Hotel Nostalgia × The Hotel Traveler** (highest intent, lowest funnel)

### Seasonal Priority:
Thoughtful Gifting × The Gift Buyer (Q4, Mother's Day, Valentine's Day)

### Retargeting / Upsell (25% of spend):
Effortless Ambiance × The Upgrader (existing customers → HVAC units)
