# Hotel Collection — Product Reference

## Company Overview

Hotel Collection is a luxury home fragrance and lifestyle brand positioned around "Inspired by 5-Star Stays." Sister brand to Aroma360 (same parent company). ~45 employees. Positioned as the more accessible, consumer-facing brand ("Gap" to Aroma360's "Banana Republic").

**CONFIDENTIAL:** Same parent also owns Hotel Scent (mass-market tier). CEO directive: all three must appear as independent competitors. Never reference parent company connection in creative.

**Scale:** 102,738 monthly organic traffic, 26,032 ranking keywords, Authority Score 43. Top organic competitor: Aroma360 (2,408 shared keywords). Other competitors: Scentiment, AromaTech, Pura, The Magic Scent, Nest New York.

**Attribution:** Triple Whale for DTC measurement.
**Ad Accounts:** Google Ads + Meta Ads. Running Google on 52 paid keywords (~$10,747/month paid traffic cost).

---

## Business Model

**Subscription-driven.** Entry-level diffusers (especially Studio Pro) are acquisition loss leaders designed to convert customers into recurring oil subscribers. Once in the ecosystem, customers are upsold to larger HVAC-connected diffusers (Villa, Penthouse, Presidential) with ongoing oil subscription commitments.

**Creative Focus Priority (~75% of spend/effort):**
- Studio Pro Scent Diffuser
- The Starter Kit
- Chauffeur Car Diffuser

These are the main acquisition engine for new customers. Remaining ~25% covers larger diffusers and lifestyle products.

## Subscription Structure (for compliance awareness)

### Studio Pro (Entry-Level Sub)
- $49.95 with 1 oil/month subscription
- 3-month minimum commitment
- Cancel in first 30 days: pay $50 difference
- Cancel after 30 days: buy remaining committed oils then cancel

### Quarterly Oil Subscription
- 3 bottles same scent every 3 months for $115.40 + shipping
- Can swap scents for future shipments
- Cancel anytime before next billing
- Unopened bottles exchangeable

### First Class Membership
- $99 annual payment (charged after 3rd day of purchase)
- Full year charged upfront, non-refundable
- Benefits continue until term ends

### VIP Oil Program (Larger Diffusers)
- 36-month commitment
- ~$150/month
- 30-day cancellation period
- Reviewed during enrollment call + signed agreement
