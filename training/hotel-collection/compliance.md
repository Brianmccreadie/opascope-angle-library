# Hotel Collection — Compliance Rules

## CRITICAL: Read Before Every Brief

---

## Evergreen Creative Direction (CLIENT DIRECTIVE)

**Hotel Collection is shifting away from price-heavy promotional creative toward evergreen creative that does NOT aggressively call out price within the product or creative itself.**

This means:
- ❌ Do NOT lead with discount percentages ("85% OFF!")
- ❌ Do NOT make price the hero of the ad
- ❌ Do NOT use aggressive strikethrough pricing as the primary hook
- ❌ Do NOT build concepts entirely around sale/deal energy
- ✅ DO focus on the sensory experience, lifestyle transformation, and hotel-inspired positioning
- ✅ DO lead with the scent story, the compliments angle, the 5-star experience
- ✅ Price can appear as a supporting element (e.g., in a CTA or end card) but should not be the concept driver
- ✅ If price is mentioned, frame it as accessible luxury — not clearance/deal energy

---

## Forbidden Words

- ❌ **"Government"** — Do not reference government in any context
- ❌ **"Official"** — Do not imply official hotel partnerships unless explicitly licensed
- ❌ **"Guaranteed"** — Avoid absolute guarantee claims
- ❌ **"Always," "Never," "100%"** — No absolute claims
- ❌ **"Cure," "Heal," "Treat," "Therapy"** — No medical/wellness claims for scents
- ❌ **"Aromatherapy"** — Avoid this term in ad creative (implies therapeutic benefit). Note: The brand uses "aromatherapy" on-site, but for paid ads avoid it to prevent platform issues.
- ❌ **"Chemical-free"** — Everything is chemicals; use "clean ingredients" or "pure luxury fragrances" instead
- ❌ **"Aroma360," "Hotel Scent"** — NEVER reference sister brands or parent company

---

## Forbidden Claims

### Pricing & Discount Claims
- ❌ Do NOT amplify discount percentages that may be challenged (e.g., "85% off" when the MSRP may never have been the real selling price)
- ❌ Do NOT use strikethrough pricing as the hero element (FTC requires "original price" to be a price at which product was actually sold for a reasonable period)
- ❌ Do NOT say "FREE" without clearly and prominently disclosing the subscription commitment
- ❌ Do NOT bury the subscription requirement — if mentioning "free diffuser," the oil subscription condition must be visible, not just in fine print
- ❌ Do NOT hide commitment periods, minimum purchases, or cancellation fees

### Product Claims
- ❌ Do NOT claim specific hotel partnerships unless using the "Inspired by" framing with ® symbol
- ❌ Do NOT say "Same scent as [Hotel]" — say "Inspired by [Hotel]®"
- ❌ Do NOT make health benefit claims for any scent products
- ❌ Do NOT claim "eliminates" odors — use "transforms your space" or "fills your home"
- ❌ Do NOT claim the diffuser is "silent" or "noiseless" (some customer complaints about noise)

### Competitor Claims
- ❌ Do NOT name competitors directly in creative
- ❌ Do NOT reference Aroma360, Pura, AromaTech, Nest, or any competitor by name
- ❌ Do NOT compare pricing to specific competitors

## Allowed Claims & Language

### Brand Positioning (Safe to Use)
- ✅ "Inspired by 5-Star Hotels"
- ✅ "Hotel-inspired"
- ✅ "Inspired by [Hotel Name]®" — with ® symbol on hotel name
- ✅ "Bring the hotel experience home"
- ✅ "Turn your home into a 5-star retreat"
- ✅ "Luxury hotel scent for your home"
- ✅ "The scent of your favorite hotel"

### Product Claims (Safe to Use)
- ⚠️ **Studio Pro color: BLACK ONLY** — Do not reference other colors (Space Gray, Champagne, etc.) in briefs. The Studio scent diffuser is currently only sold in black.
- ✅ Specific review counts and ratings (Studio Pro: 2,484+ reviews at 4.9/5)
- ✅ "Scents up to [X] sq ft" — technical capacity claim
- ✅ "Cold-air diffusion technology"
- ✅ "Waterless, heatless"
- ✅ "No residue"
- ✅ "Safe for children and pets"
- ✅ "Cruelty-free"
- ✅ "Phthalate-free"
- ✅ "Made in the USA"
- ✅ "Pure luxury fragrances"
- ✅ Licensed partnerships (Star Wars, NBA)

### Subscription (If Referenced — Must Include Disclosure)
- ✅ "Get the diffuser with oil deliveries" — but must disclose commitment terms
- ✅ "Monthly scent deliveries" — as convenience framing
- ✅ "New scent every month" — experiential framing
- ⚠️ If mentioning "free" in any context, subscription terms must be clearly disclosed in the ad itself

### Sensory & Experiential Language (Encouraged)
- ✅ "Transform your space"
- ✅ "Walk in to your favorite hotel scent"
- ✅ "The first thing your guests will notice"
- ✅ "Fills your home with luxury"
- ✅ "Elevate your everyday"
- ✅ Scent note descriptions (e.g., "sandalwood and leather," "pomegranate and peony")
- ✅ Hotel atmosphere descriptions (lobbies, suites, spas)

## Platform-Specific Restrictions

### Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
- No known restrictions on home fragrance ads
- ⚠️ **Wine products:** Alcohol advertising policies apply — age 21+ targeting required in US, additional copy restrictions
- Standard text-in-image guidelines apply
- Subscription terms should be visible, not buried

### Google Ads
- No known category restrictions on home fragrance
- ⚠️ **Wine products:** Alcohol advertising policies apply
- Landing page must match ad claims

### TikTok
- Native content style preferred
- No known category restrictions on home fragrance
- ⚠️ **Wine products:** Alcohol advertising restrictions apply

## Hotel Trademark Disclaimer

The following disclaimer appears on Hotel Collection's website and should be noted for compliance awareness:

*"HotelCollection® is not affiliated, associated, authorized or endorsed by any of the following hotel or hotel groups or any subsidiaries or affiliates thereof."*

In creative:
- Always use "Inspired by [Hotel Name]®" — never imply direct affiliation
- Do NOT use hotel logos
- Do NOT say "official scent of" or "as used by"
- The ® symbol should appear on the hotel name being referenced

## Pre-Brief Compliance Checklist

Before finalizing ANY brief, verify:

- [ ] No aggressive price/discount as the primary hook
- [ ] No absolute claims (always, never, 100%, guaranteed)
- [ ] No health/wellness/therapeutic claims for scents
- [ ] No competitor names mentioned
- [ ] No sister brand references (Aroma360, Hotel Scent)
- [ ] Hotel names use "Inspired by" framing with ®
- [ ] Any "free" claims include visible subscription disclosure
- [ ] Any subscription mentions include commitment terms
- [ ] No implied hotel affiliation/endorsement
- [ ] Wine ads (if applicable) include age-gating notes
- [ ] Evergreen creative direction honored — not price-led
