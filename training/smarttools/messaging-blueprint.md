### Key Talking Points
1. **Value disruption:** SmartCuffs delivers the same clinical results as a $5,000 Delphi — proven in peer-reviewed research — for $499
2. **The only one:** Only USA-made Bluetooth auto-calibrating BFR device at this price
3. **Joint protection:** Build muscle with 30% of the weight — your joints will thank you

### Must Avoids ⛔
- ❌ **No medical claims about treating or curing conditions** — BFR is a training tool, not a medical treatment
- ❌ **No FDA clearance claims** unless specifically verified for SmartCuffs (competitors like Delphi have FDA Class III; SmartCuffs positioning is different)
- ❌ **No LeBron James content without permission** — He uses BFR generally, not a SmartCuffs endorser. Say "used by professional athletes" instead.
- ❌ **No injury recovery promises** — Reference the science, don't promise outcomes. "BFR research shows..." not "BFR will fix your knee"
- ❌ **No claiming Peter Attia is a paid endorser** — His use is organic; don't imply a formal partnership
- ❌ **No bashing cheap bands as dangerous** — Position SmartCuffs as "precision BFR" without fear-mongering about alternatives causing harm
- ❌ **No "before and after" medical claims** without proper disclaimers
- ❌ **No overstating BFR as superior to heavy lifting** — It produces *comparable* results, not better. The advantage is lower load on joints.
