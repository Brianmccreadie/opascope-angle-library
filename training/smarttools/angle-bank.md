## How to Use This Bank

1. **Pick an angle** that matches your campaign objective and target segment
2. **Stack it** with a characteristic segment from the Segment Plan for precise targeting
3. **Test 3–5 hooks** from each angle before declaring a winner or loser
4. **Rotate angles** every 2–4 weeks to prevent creative fatigue
5. **Tag results** back to this bank — mark angles as Tested / Winner / Fatigued
