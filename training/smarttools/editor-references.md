## 🎬 Editor Notes

Videos are sorted by total impressions (highest first). Review for: hook style (first 3 seconds), pacing/cut frequency, text overlay approach, music/audio choice, CTA placement, aspect ratio (9:16 vs 1:1), UGC vs produced, and B2B vs B2C messaging. The brand runs heavy on testimonial/UGC content (~40% of their library) with a mix of clinical/PT-focused and lifestyle training creative.

## 📝 Editor Takeaways

1. Review the first 3 seconds of each video — this is the hook. Note which hooks stop the scroll.
2. Pay attention to pacing — how many cuts per second, when does the edit breathe vs. rapid-fire.
3. Note text overlay style — font, size, position, animation, timing.
4. Identify the audio strategy — voiceover, music-only, natural sound, or mixed.
5. Track CTA placement — when does the call-to-action appear and how is it formatted.
6. Note aspect ratios — which videos are 9:16 (Stories/Reels) vs 1:1 (Feed).
7. Look for UGC vs. produced content — which style dominates and which performs (higher impression = higher in the list).
8. Identify B2B vs B2C creative — clinical/PT-focused ads have different editing styles than consumer fitness ads.
9. The highest-impression videos (Group 1) represent their best-performing creative — study these closest.
10. Look for patterns in video length — shorter videos for awareness, longer for education/consideration.
